## üé® Concept 1: Enlightened Diya (Recommended)

**File:** `concept1-enlightened-diya.svg`

### Description
A stylized traditional Indian oil lamp (diya) with a flame that incorporates subtle AI circuit patterns. The flame curves in an "S" shape, subtly referencing "Shikshan."

### Symbolism
- **Diya (‡§¶‡•Ä‡§Ø‡§æ):** Traditional Indian symbol of knowledge and enlightenment
- **Flame:** The light of education dispelling darkness of ignorance
- **Circuit nodes:** AI technology powering modern learning
- **S-curve:** Initial of ShikshanAI

### Why This Works
1. **Cultural Connection:** Diya is universally recognized in India across all religions
2. **Educational Meaning:** "Tamaso ma jyotirgamaya" (Lead me from darkness to light) - Upanishadic wisdom
3. **Modern + Traditional:** Bridges ancient Indian education values with AI technology
4. **Unique:** No major edtech competitor uses this symbol
5. **Scalable:** Works at all sizes from favicon to billboard

### Color Palette
- Primary: Indigo `#4F46E5` to Purple `#7C3AED` gradient
- Accent: Amber/Gold `#F59E0B` (flame glow)
- Base: White/Light for contrast

### Best Used For
- App icon (primary recommendation)
- Splash screen
- Marketing materials
- Brand identity

## ü¶â Concept 2: Wisdom Owl

**File:** `concept2-wisdom-owl.svg`

### Description
A friendly, geometric owl wearing a graduation cap. One eye has a normal pupil while the other contains a subtle circuit pattern, representing the blend of natural wisdom and AI.

### Symbolism
- **Owl:** Universal symbol of wisdom and knowledge
- **Graduation Cap:** Academic achievement, education
- **Circuit Eye:** AI-powered intelligence
- **Friendly Expression:** Approachable for young students

### Cultural Note
In Indian mythology, the owl (Ullu) is the vahana (vehicle) of Goddess Lakshmi. While sometimes associated with foolishness in colloquial usage, in educational context it represents wisdom (like Athena's owl in Greek mythology).

### Why This Works
1. **Mascot Potential:** Can be animated, used in gamification
2. **Kid-Friendly:** Approachable for Class 9-10 students
3. **Memorable:** Duolingo proved owl mascots work for education
4. **Versatile:** Can show different expressions/poses

### Color Palette
- Body: Indigo `#4F46E5` to Purple `#7C3AED` gradient
- Face: Light indigo `#E0E7FF`
- Cap: Dark gray `#1F2937`
- Beak/Tassel: Amber `#F59E0B`

### Best Used For
- App icon
- Mascot for gamification
- Social media
- Merchandise
